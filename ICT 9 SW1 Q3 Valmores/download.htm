osu!mania



SYNOPSIS

This game is a vertical scroll rhythm game that is similar to the likes of Dance Dance Revolution however, instead of using the pad, you are using a keyboard. You can pretty much download any song you want with different mappers (the people who make the patterns of the notes in the song) and song artists. If it exists, you can play it. You can set the keybinds of each note to whatever you want. Mappers may even add in special effects, also known as a storyboard, which makes playing the song, all the more interesting. They may also add videos in the background to add more entertainment, especially if you have spectators watching you play your favorite songs.
